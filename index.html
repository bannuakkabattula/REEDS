About REEDS Uplifting Rural Lives with Purpose.

Since 1991, Rural Economical Education and Development Society (REEDS) has been a driving force in fostering rural education, economic empowerment, and sustainable development across Andhra Pradesh. As a registered NGO, we work towards creating self-sufficient communities, equipping them with knowledge, skills, and resources to thrive in an evolving world.

Our Impact

With over three decades of service, REEDS has transformed the lives of thousands in Andhra Pradesh. Our achievements include:

  • Increased literacy rates through school enrollment initiatives.
  • Enhanced employment via vocational & entrepreneurship training.
  • Improved healthcare access via medical camps & awareness programs.
  • Supported rural infrastructure for sustainable community development
